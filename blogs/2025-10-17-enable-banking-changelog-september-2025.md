September’s Enable Banking Changelog introduces major updates for TPPs and 
developers. Explore our new TPP Infrastructure-as-a-Service documentation, 
which outlines how to operate under your own authorisation with full PSD2, 
GDPR, and DORA compliance. We’ve also expanded our Market Specifics section 
to cover all EU countries — including Norway, Romania, Malta, and 
Luxembourg — and launched a new smart certificate management tool to 
simplify renewals. Plus, new integrations with Artea (LT) and Banca Widiba 
(IT) are now live.
