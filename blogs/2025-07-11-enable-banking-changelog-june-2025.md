The Enable Banking June 2025 changelog introduces country-specific open 
banking insight pages, helping developers navigate market-level differences 
in SCA flows, payment types, and ASPSP behavior with less guesswork. We 
also launched automated emails for application status updates and added new 
integrations, including Alisa Bank and HSBC, alongside fixes across 40+ 
existing ASPSPs. Designed for technical teams, these updates aim to 
streamline decision-making, speed up implementation, and improve 
transparency.
