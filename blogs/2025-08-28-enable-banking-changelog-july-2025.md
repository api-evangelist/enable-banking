In July 2025, Enable Banking expanded its open banking coverage to over 
2,500 ASPSPs across Europe. New integrations were added in Austria, 
Croatia, and Finland—including a new PSD2 API from Aktia with extended 
consent validity. We also delivered improvements across more than 40 
existing bank integrations, strengthening reliability and performance. 
These updates highlight our commitment to expanding coverage, improving 
data quality, and supporting TPPs with robust, production-ready 
connections.
