The May 2025 Enable Banking changelog introduces country-level ASPSP status 
notifications, public guides for hosting the eIDAS broker microservice on 
Azure and AWS, and expanded integration coverage, including new support for 
Artea, Facit Bank, SDC (with 1 SCA flow), and Wise. We’ve also rolled out 
fixes and enhancements across 30+ bank integrations in multiple regions.
