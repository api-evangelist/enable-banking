This month brought a new deferred payment submission flow that lets your application run checks for fraud prevention or internal compliance before a payment is finalised at the ASPSP. UI widgets also migrated to a new origin, with a January 2027 deprecation timeline worth knowing about. You'll also find eight new integrations, five extended with new capabilities (card accounts in Italy, decoupled auth in Sweden, PIS at Svea Bank), reliability improvements across more than fifty ASPSPs, and a couple of brand consolidations in the Polish and Spanish markets.
