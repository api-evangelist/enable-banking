At Enable Banking, we built an internal AI chatbot that lets our team talk 
to our documentation. Using a Retrieval-Augmented Generation (RAG) setup, 
we taught AI to fetch the right answers from our docs, fast, accurate, and 
with real business value. Here’s how we did it, what we learned, and why we 
believe AI can make internal knowledge tools both smarter and more human.
