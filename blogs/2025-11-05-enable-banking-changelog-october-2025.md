In October, we introduced a new endpoint for manual payment deletion, 
expanded PIS and AIS coverage, and extended default consent validity to 180 
days.

We’re also launching live demo sessions for the Control Panel, giving users 
hands-on guidance for debugging, monitoring, and managing their 
integrations.
